FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

A static website is typically ready in 3–5 working days. A dynamic or e-commerce website takes 7–14 working days. We commit to a clear deadline at the start — and we honour it.

Are domain and hosting included in the price?

Domain and hosting are billed separately and are not included in the design fee. These are third-party costs you pay directly to providers like GoDaddy or Hostinger. We guide you through the entire setup. Annual renewal is approximately ₹1,500–₹3,500/year depending on your chosen plan.

How does payment work? Is an advance required?

We work on a 50% advance to begin and 50% on delivery. We accept UPI, bank transfer, and other standard payment methods. No pressure — review the design first, and pay only when you're happy to proceed.

What if I need changes after the website is delivered?

You get 1 full year of free support post-delivery. Minor updates (text, images) are completely free. Larger changes (new pages, additional features) are handled at a nominal cost. Just WhatsApp us — we typically respond within the hour.

Do you work with clients outside Noida?

Absolutely. We serve clients across India — Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, and beyond — as well as international clients. The entire process is handled online, seamlessly.

What types of websites do you build?

We build all types of websites — static brochure sites for small businesses, dynamic websites with admin panels, e-commerce stores with payment gateways, restaurant websites with menus, coaching institute portals, real estate listing sites, medical/clinic websites, and multi-vendor marketplaces. If you have a specific requirement, just WhatsApp us and we'll guide you.

Will my website rank on Google?

Every website we deliver is built with on-page SEO best practices — clean code, fast load speed, proper meta tags, heading structure, and mobile responsiveness. These are the foundations Google rewards. For higher rankings, we also offer dedicated SEO services. Most of our clients start receiving organic Google traffic within 30–90 days of going live.

Can I update my website content myself?

Yes! Dynamic websites (Standard & Premium plans) come with an easy-to-use admin panel where you can update text, images, products, and more without any technical knowledge. For static websites, small changes are handled free of cost by our team — just WhatsApp us.

Do you provide a GST invoice?

We currently do not provide a GST invoice. We are a small independent studio and are not GST registered. Payments are accepted via UPI or bank transfer, and a basic payment receipt is provided on request. If GST billing is a strict requirement for your business, please let us know before starting.
